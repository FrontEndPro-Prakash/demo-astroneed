How can I improve Health and Wealth?

Health of a native depends upon the placement of the Ascendant lord and lord of the 6 th house in the chart and its conjunction or aspects with each other and/or lord of 12 th House. The placement of planets in 6 th house also plays a major role in the health of the native. Timing of same can be calculated based on Dasha, Bhukti & antra of the planets signifying the Asc, 6 th , 8 th & 12 th houses.

Wealth is mainly decided by the 2 nd & 11 th houses & their lords in combination with the Ascendant, 6 th & 7 th houses & their lords. Planets signifying their combination will result in the type & quantum of wealth the native will attain during his/her life.

Which line of Education suits me?
The intelligence is decided by the 5 th house, its lord & placement of Mercury in the birth Chart. However, higher education is judged from the 4 th & 9 th houses and their lords. Thus, their placements & running Dasha, Bhukti plays a major role in deciding whether the native will have good education or not & type of stream also
When will my Financial Difficulties over?
Astrologically the 2 nd house in birth chart signifies finance mainly. Gains through speculative activity is linked with 5 th house, 8 th house represents gains from inheritance and 11 th house signifies monetary gains from own labour. Placement of ruler of these houses in chart and the present vimshotari dasha Bhukti & Antara determines when the financial difficulties will be over and how. Remedial solutions help in overcoming them.
How I want to improve my financial health?
Strengthening the 2 nd and 11 th houses and their lords and the planets signifying these houses and analyzing the strengths of 5 th & 8 th houses and placement of Venus, Moon and Jupiter in the birth chart with weakening the 12 th house, leads to improvement in the financial health of the native. Off course Dasha Bhukti & Antara dasha do plays a major role regarding timing.
When will I get Married?
Marriage leads to addition to family by a third person where native has or develops affection / Love. The ruling houses for it are 2 nd (addition to family), 5 th (Love or affection), 7 th (spouse) and 11 th (fulfilment of wishes). When the native runs the Dasha, Bhukti, Antar and Pratyantra of these houses, he/she gets married.
When will I have my own house?
The house / flat or landed property or vehicle is represented by the 4 th house and planets significator for it are Mars, Saturn and Mercury. The 11 th house leads to success or fulfilment of wishes. 12 th house represents expenses and 6 th house shows Loans or borrowing for the purchase of it. Therefore, during the Dasha, Bhukti, antra & Pratyantar dashas of the planets signifying the above, leads to owning of them.
